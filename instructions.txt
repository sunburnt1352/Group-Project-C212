Welcome to our ASL Fingerspelling Program! We hope you enjoy learning
finger signs with our game.

To run the program, simply run "Main.java".

You can choose between two game modes by interacting with the menu screen.
The first is Letter Mode, where you can learn (or practice) the individual
letters. Type the right letter to get points! The second is Word Mode,
where you'll see a sequence of letters and must type the word.

The game continues until you return to the menu or make 3 mistakes. Your
results will be written to a daily output file!

Enjoy our ASL game!
